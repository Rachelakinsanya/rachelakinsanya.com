About Rachel

I didn't come from tech.
I came from needing more with less.

"I run three businesses simultaneously. All operate on a 20-hour week. That's only possible because of the systems I've built. I'm not a developer. I came from needing to do more with less time — and figured out that the right system, built intentionally, is the most practical way to get there."
